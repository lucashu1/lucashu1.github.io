Lucas Who?

I am Trustee Scholar and Viterbi Fellow at the University of Southern California (USC). I major in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics, and plan to graduate in the Spring of 2020 with an M.S. in E.E. (Specialization: Data Science). I work in Dr. Tambe's Center for Artificial Intelligence in Society (CAIS), and lead the Student Branch of the Center for A.I. in Society (CAIS++).
